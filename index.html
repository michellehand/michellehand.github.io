About

Journalist instinct. Analyst mindset.

I came into analytics through healthcare, but my approach has always been shaped by a journalist’s instinct—asking better questions, looking for context, and trying to understand what’s actually happening beneath the surface.

My work focuses on the intersection of behavior, environment, physiology, and measurable outcomes—whether in healthcare, personal data, outdoor environments, or digital systems.

I’m interested in questions where the data is incomplete and the context matters—where understanding behavior requires more than just the numbers.

Background

12+ years in healthcare · 5+ years in analytics · experience across claims data, cohort design, outcome comparison, and stakeholder-facing insight.

Formal training in business analytics, with continued coursework in statistics, calculus, and linear algebra to deepen quantitative rigor.
